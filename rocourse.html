  • Robust discrete optimization
    Proposed for the academic year 2024-2025
  • Instructors: Ayse N. Arslan and Michael Poss.
  • Content
    • This course will focus on a class of optimization under uncertainty approaches in discrete optimization. This approaches aim to solve optimization problems plagued by uncertainty in some of their parameters
    • To do so, we will rely on integer programming and associated algorithms such as branch-and-bound as a framework to model and solve NP-Hard discrete optimization problems.
    • In order to treat uncertainty in this context we will rely on the robust optimization framework where uncertain parameters are assumed to belong to polytopes for which an outer description is available.
