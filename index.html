Current position

Junior Researcher (CR) at Centre Inria de l'Universite de Bordeaux

Research interests
  • Mathematical programming with emphasis on mixed integer programming under uncertainty (both stochastic and robust optimization).
  • Development of theory and methodology for solving these problems with a special focus on decomposition methods and polyhedral approaches.
  • Application of solution methodologies to industrial problems, in areas including but not limited to production planning, supply chain management, healthcare, transportation science and energy management.
